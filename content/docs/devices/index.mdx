FrameOS ships with **120+ display drivers**, selectable when you add a frame in the backend.
Three generic drivers cover everything with a video output, and dedicated SPI drivers cover most
e-ink panels by Waveshare and Pimoroni — from 1" badges to 13.3" full-color Spectra 6 panels.

## Notes

- **Web only** runs a frame with no physical display — view it in the browser. Great for trying
  FrameOS, or for kiosk setups where another machine displays the image.
- **HTTP upload** pushes each rendered image to a URL of your choosing — drive displays FrameOS
  has never heard of.
- The **Pimoroni Inky** panels have native Nim drivers for recent models and a Python fallback
  driver for everything else.
- Displays not on this list: open an issue, or try the closest driver — controller boards are
  often shared between panels.
